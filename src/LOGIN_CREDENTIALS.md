## Feature Access by Role

### Administrator Features:
✅ Full dashboard with all statistics
✅ View/add/edit/remove all members
✅ Approve/reject member signups
✅ Manage contributions
✅ View detailed reports (including member-specific data)
✅ Send notifications via WhatsApp
✅ Manage expenses
✅ System settings

### Member Features:
✅ Personal dashboard with their own stats
✅ View their profile information
✅ View their contribution history
✅ View generalized financial reports (no individual member details)
✅ See organization-level statistics

❌ Cannot view other members
❌ Cannot manage contributions
❌ Cannot send notifications
❌ Cannot access admin settings
